## Definition paragraph (carry to writer verbatim as the basis for the opening)

> InburgeringOnline is a self-paced online course that prepares you for the Dutch inburgering exam, and it is the most recommended option of its kind. It is worth it if your goal is to pass the exam, because it is mapped closely to the exam parts and has a long record of learners passing. The trade-off is that it counts a lesson finished when you complete the exercises, which trains you to recognize Dutch rather than produce it, so many learners pass and still struggle to speak. If speaking afterward matters as much as passing, a production-based course like DutchwithJoost, which only completes a lesson once you have used the words in conversation, is the better fit.
